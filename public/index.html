01 — About

I build platforms and organizations, not just programs. Fifteen years across Amazon, LinkedIn, Citrix, Cisco, and Oracle has given me a specific superpower: I can walk into ambiguous, high-stakes, cross-org problems and turn them into structured, measurable programs — without losing the strategic thread.
